MP board 10th and 12th examination update

A special notice by MPBSE for 10th and 12th students of MP board. There is relief news for the students who are worried about the 10th and 12th examination of MP board. Sources have shown that MP board exam 2021 for 10th and 12th classes would likely begin from end of April 2021 and go on till may 2021. Usually ,MP board exams used to start in the last week of February but this year year due to corona epidemic, academic year of students start too much late therefore MPBSE has decided that this year examinations will start a little late so that is student can get sufficient time to prepare for their exams.

Examination pattern:-
The MP government is also considering doing away with ‘supplementary examination’ and instead conduct board examination in two session. MPBSE conducted a meeting to discuss various possibilities about the pattern of examination. Among the many suggestions it was decided to delete the class tenth and twelfth board examination by two months to a date in April and the board is also considering conducting the board examinations twice this year. The second board examination would be conducted after a gap of 3 months and students who fail in the first session can appear for the examination in the second session. Much like a supplementary examination, the difference is that the second session would be considered as the main examination.
Also, with the pandemic situation, the board is considering conducting the examinations online. To begin with, MPBSE would conduct the MP Class 10, 12 pre board examinations online. Based on the result and success of the online examination, the board would then decide on conducting the examination online this year.
There has also been a big change in exam pattern, this year 30% of paper will be objective type.
We should notice hear that whatever decision is being taken by MPBSE, thinking about the safety of his students because every year about 15 to 20 lakh students conduct the tenth and twelfth board exams. Therefore ,it becomes our duty to accept their decision and continue our preparation for examination.

Update of SSC examination:-
On the other hand, Staff Selection Commission (SSC) also reschedule the exam dates for all SSC exams to be conducted in the upcoming months. The exam dates for SSC CHSL Tier 1, SSC JE Paper 1, SSC CGL Tier 2, SSC CPO Paper 1, and other exams have been postponed due to the prevailing COVID-19 conditions. Check out the SSC Calendar 2020-21 with the revised exam dates for all upcoming exams.
SSC has released SSC CHSL and SSC CGL skill test dates on 1st october 2020. According to the official notice, SSC CHSL and SSC CGL skill tests will be conducted in November and December respectively. Due to the prevailing Covid-19 and the Commission has decided to conduct the skill test on pan India basis.
