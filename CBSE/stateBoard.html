CBSE board examination update:
A big news is came from education minister for those students who are preparing for 10th and 12th CBSE board examination. After analysing the situation of pandemic in States union education minister Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank on Thursday December 31 announce that CBSE class 10th and 12th board exam 2021 will be held from may 4 to June 10 and results will be declared by July 15.

If seen, every year board exams used to start from month of February and end in march , and results were declared by month of May but because of coronavirus academic year of 2020 -2021 started a bit late and classes were held online.

Dr. Ramesh pokhriyal nishank also announced that practical exams will begin from first week of March. He clarified that there is no possibility of conducting board examination till the end of February month. He also said that the board exams will be offline as usual and not online.

This update by education minister is very helpful for those children who are confused and anxious about the dates of examination after by this update on exams children should keep studying continuously and keep preparing themselves for the board examination. At that time parents also have an obligation to their children's not to worry about exams and to encourage their children's towards their studies. Also childrens who are happy board exams and they get general promotion, then they should not have such a wrong fame.

On other side many students and engineering aspirant have been waiting patiently for the officials to announce the detail regarding the upcoming IIT JEE exam. National testing agency (NTA);which conduct the JEE main has released a new update for JEE Main 2021 exam on its official portals.

According to newly update, the registration process for JEE main 2021 is starting from 16th December 2020.Therefore students who are interested can now visit the official website (jeemain.nta.nic.in) and start feeling the JEE main application form. January 16th 2021 is the last date of submission of application form for JEE mains exam, while the application fees can be paid by January 17th and correction in the forms starting from 19th January and its windows remains open till January 21, 2021. Admit cards for JEE mains exam will released in the second week of February.

This exam will held in many sessions which are as follows:

  • In session 1, exam will held on 23,24 25 and 26 February of 2021.
  • In session 2, exam will held on 15 ,16 ,17 and18 March of 2021.
  • In session 3, exam will held on 27, 28, 29 and 30 April of 2021.
  • In session 4, exam will held on 24, 25, 26, 27 and 28 May of 2021.
