Covid-19

Covid-19 disease is a pestilential disease. It is caused by newly discover lethal corona virus which can transmit from person to person. This virus affects your upper respiratory tract (sinuses, nose and throat) or lower respiratory tract (windpipe and lungs). There are several types of corona viruses except SARS-Cov-2 other corona viruses cause most of the colds that affect us during the year, but they are not a serious danger otherwise healthy person. SARS-Cov-2 one of type of corona viruses which cause severe diseases like Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Sudden Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

Origin

Covid-19 is a highly transmittable and pathogenic viral infection. Its first case found in wuhan town of china in December 2019. By genomic analysis it is revealed that SARS-Cov-2 is like SARS virus which is known as bat viruses that by bat could be the primary reservoir. But exact origin of this virus still a mystery for us.


SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19

A healthy person is not much at risk from covid-19. It is more dangerous for old and sick people symptoms starts appearing within five to fourteen days after infected by covid-19.


Some of the following symptoms appear in body infected by covid-19:


MOST COMMON SYMPTOMS
  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Tiredness
SERIOUS SYMPTOMS
  • Difficulty in breathing or shortness in breathing
  • Chest pain
  • Having trouble in speaking and movement
LESS COMMON SYMPTOMS
  • Aches and sore throat
  • Diarrhea
  • Headache and loss of taste

⭐ Covid-19 affects different people in diverse way. Most infected people develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization


Precautions

As the vaccine of covid-19 has not yet been fully discovered therefore it is our duty to protect ourselves from covid-19

  • Create a social distancing at crowded places.
  • Keep your hands sanitized.
  • Do not touch doors and railing at crowded places.
  • Do not touch your eyes ,nose or mouth.
  • Get out of the houses only when there is some important work.
  • If you have a fever and difficulty in breathing, seek medical attention.
  • Quarantine yourself if you see symptoms of covid—19.
